# Iris layout

Tailored for Norwegian mappings, might look unfamiliar.


## Main layer

NAV and SYM both act as Enter when tapped.

```

,----+----+----+----+----+----.              ,----+----+----+----+----+----.
| |§ | 1  | 2  | 3  | 4  | 5  |                6  | 7  | 8  | 9  | 0  | +? |
|----+----+----+----+----+----|              |----+----+----+----+----+----|
| TAB| Q  | W  | E  | R  | T  |                Y  | U  | I  | O  | P  | Å  |
|----+----+----+----+----+----|              |----+----+----+----+----+----|
|CESC| A  | S  | D  | F  | G  |                H  | J  | K  | L  | Ø  | Æ  |
|----+----+----+----+----+----+----.    ,----|----+----+----+----+----+----|
|SFLK| Z  | X  | C  | V  | B  |MUTE|    |BSPC| K  | M  | ,; | .: | -_ |RSFT|
`----+----+----+--+-+----+----+----/    \----+----+----+----+----+----+----'
                  |LGUI| NAV| SPC /      \ SPC | SYM|LALT|
                  `----+----+----'        `----+----+----'
```

## Gaming layer

Left part is shifted one row to the right to put WASD in a natural position.

```
,----+----+----+----+----+----.              ,----+----+----+----+----+----.
|MAIN| 1  | 2  | 3  | 4  | 5  |                6  | 7  | 8  | 9  | 0  | __ |
|----+----+----+----+----+----|              |----+----+----+----+----+----|
| TAB| ESC| Q  | W  | E  | R  |                Y  | U  | I  | O  | P  | __ |
|----|----+----+----+----+----+              |----+----+----+----+----+----|
| T  |LSFT| A  | S  | D  | F  |                H  | J  | K  | L  | T  |NGAM|
|----+----+----+----+----+----+----.    ,----|----+----+----+----+----+----|
| M  |LCTL| Z  | X  | C  | V  | __ |    | __ | N  | M  | G  | B  | __ |AGAM|
`----+----+----+--+-+----+----+----/    \----+----+----+----+----+----+----'
                  |LGUI|LALT| SPC /      \  __ | __ | __ |
                  `----+----+----'        `----+----+----'
```

Pressing the layer keys to the far right toggles arrow keys or nav cluster in the IJKL position.
